Frequent Q&A

What is Viewz studio?
It is a Web AR, an app-less augmented reality content creating solution, that enables you to augment your products instantly without the need for app downloading or coding knowledge. They can be integrated to any web browser. Users can access web AR by clicking a link or scanning a QR code to open the items on your website.

Do I need any coding skills to use Viewz?
Not at all, all you have to do is to drag and drop your 2D images, test, adjust, publish, and you are done.

Can I publish AR experience for free?
Yes, you can get a basic plan for this. This license is limited to prototypes and demonstrations (it cannot be used for commercial purposes) for 15 days only. To publish an experience for commercial use, you need to subscribe to one of our packages.
