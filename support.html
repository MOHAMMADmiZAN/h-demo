Help and Support

Bring your products to life, reach new customers, and provide the best online shopping experience with Viewz webAR

What is web AR?

WebAR is a web-based augmented reality platform that enables retailers and business owners to augment their products and services in an easy and fast manner, without the need for application or a coding experience. This technology allows you to create an AR experience and let your customers access it directly from their smartphones using the native camera and mobile web browser; this immersive, enjoyable, and unique experience of AR works on most operating systems, mobile devices, and web browser

How customers can use it?

All your customers need is an internet connection, and all you need is Viewz

All they need to do is navigate to the webpage URL to view the augmented reality items you provide, and try it in their space through any iOS or Android smartphone camera; no app downloads are required

Completely app-less Augmented Reality experiences for your customers!

How can you use it?

Simply log in to create your account, create collection, upload your products’ images, then drag, drop examine and publish

After these simple steps you can send your customers to a specific URL, and voila! They are immediately interacting with your product.
