What is web AR?

WebAR is a web-based augmented reality platform that enables retailers and business owners to augment their products in an easy and fast manner, without the need for an application or a coding experience.

This technology allows you to create an AR experience and let your customers access it directly from their smartphones using the native camera and mobile web browser; this immersive, enjoyable, and unique experience of AR works on most operating systems, mobile devices, and web browser.

How customers can use it?

All your customers need is an internet connection, and all you need is Viewz

All your customers need to do is navigate to the webpage URL to view the augmented reality items you provide, and then try it in their space through any iOS or Android smartphone camera; completely app-less Augmented Reality experiences for your customers!
