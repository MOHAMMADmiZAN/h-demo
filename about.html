About Viewz

“A seamless, reliable, and efficient web AR studio for an immersive, interactive, and attractive shopping experience”

Based on our belief in the essential role of technology in the economic modern world, we decided to build a seamless, reliable, and efficient augmented reality platform for retailers, to help them promote and display their products in an immersive, interactive, and attractive way. Viewz is a SaaS (Software as a service) AR platform that converts images of art, floors, walls, furniture and products into Augmented Reality in a seamless easy way to provide you with the ultimate try-before-buy experience for your consumers & to enhance the shopping experiences at your website. Mobiles and taps are an essential part of the way consumers shop nowadays; to remain competitive, you need to do more than creating a mobile-responsive website, this exactly what Viewz is all about, it is an excellent option for Art, flooring, wall hangings, and furniture retailers looking to engage consumers across digital and physical channels. Viewz web AR can seamlessly connect with any e-commerce website to pull the image’s data of flat products and retrieve it with a 3D model and ”Try It in Your Space” experience. Also simply you can drag and drop an image to create a customized experience that generates exports, batch processing, analytics, and 3D configurators, combined with an intuitive, easy-to-use interface that will help you augment your products at scale in a snap. Viewz integrates very well with any existing marketing platforms and requires no application to download, which many consumers prefer not to do.

Industries

Art & Design
No purchasing decision is made on emotions as to buying an art piece or design. Buyers choose the piece that reflects their personal taste in their own space. With Viewz you can offer your clients the chance to view and try your art at the place or corner they wish to place it in.
Flooring products
Most consumers prefer to try the product before buying it, but when it comes to carpets or flooring products, this option is not really available; also, you do not want your carpet to be moved many times, and you can’t offer your customer the ability to see how it will look like in reality, But with Viewz, you will be able to do that, your customers will have the ability to ‘try-before-buy’ AR experience, not only for carpets, and rugs but also for tiles, parquet, and all flooring products! Viewz can convert a single tile image into an entire floor. With Viewz you can give your clients the real-time experience of the entire floor.
Wall Coverings
Provide your clients the ability to try painting, wallpaper, wall cladding, and all kinds of wall coverings in the easiest and most interactive experience with Viewz Web AR. With Viewz you have a unique feature that enables you to convert a single piece of wallpaper pattern image into a full wallpaper AR experience, which will help your clients see exactly how it would look like in reality.
Furniture industry
No more wondering or hesitation whether your product fits with your clients existing furniture or décor, transform your online furniture store into a selling machine with Viewz web AR. Furniture is not a product that is easy to move, try or return, by offering your prospects the ability to view and try your furniture in their place, you are creating a strong emotional connection with your prospects, which will convert them into customers, boost sales and reduce returns.
