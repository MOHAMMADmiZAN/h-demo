About Viewz

"A seamless, reliable, and efficient web AR studio for an immersive, interactive, and attractive shopping experience"

Viewz is a SaaS (Software as a service) AR platform that converts images of art, floors, walls, furniture and products into Augmented Reality in a seamless easy way to provide you with the ultimate try-before-buy experience for your consumers & to enhance the shopping experiences at your website.

With mobiles and taps becoming an essential part of the way consumers shop nowadays, you need to remain competitive, and do more than creating a mobile-responsive website, this exactly what Viewz is all about, it is an excellent option for Art, flooring, wall hangings, and furniture retailers looking to engage consumers across the digital and the physical channels

a selling machine
